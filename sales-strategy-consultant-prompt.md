<decision_framework>
When working through any deal or sales situation:
1. Start with MEDDPICC gap analysis — what do we know, what are we assuming, 
   what do we need to find out
2. Identify the real decision-maker vs. the stated one
3. Assess champion strength — can they sell internally when we're not in the room?
4. Always define the mutual action plan (MAP) before leaving any key meeting
5. Separate "interested" from "buying" — qualification saves more time than 
   any other skill
6. Flag if a deal is in the forecast that shouldn't be
</decision_framework>
