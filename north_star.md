This document defines the value and ideal outcomes I want to engineer for this agentic event intelligence and content pipeline system.

What I mean by “agentic event intelligence and content pipeline system” is a system that aggregates NYC AI and Agentic events so I can review them and more easily schedule my weeks. From that, generating the pre-event research on the event topics, the hosts and sponsors, the speakers, their companies, and attendees (if attendee information can be collected). From that, the research and insight are turned into pre-event content to share, from which the assets I collect at the event, transcripts, photos, and conversations, can be easily turned into post-event content, and part of job opening and networking follow-up. 

**The outcome in one sentence** — what does your life look like when this system is running well?

I am able to free myself and my mind up to focus on going deeper on the subject matter, on building, on connecting with people, because the manual and time-consuming steps of getting from 0 to 1 with my event schedule, my pre-event prep, my post-event content, and my follow-up is reliability systemized and simplified.

**Your hard constraints** — time budget per week, tools you will and won't use, things that must be manual vs. automated.

Where at all possible, sticking to the tools I have in STACK\_README.md

Many of the tools are paid pro services that should support the necessary connectivity, integrations, and functionality to support this use case and scale to more complex extensions of this foundation, as well as alternative supportive use cases.

**Success metrics** — how will you know each pipeline stage is working?

Using the resources created about upcoming events, I always have a full week of events, at least 2 events with approved registration, coming into the week, not still scrambling during the week to find and try and fill out my schedule.

If every minute of research I do about the events, companies, hosts, sponsors, and speakers is additive but unnecessary. The research is comprehensive, and the content is post-ready. Similarly, the after-event content is ready to share if I didn’t touch it, which frees me up to bring more style and personalization if I so choose.   

As a result of the better conversations at events, more consistent and quality content production, if I see a \+100% growth in engagement and an uptick in high quality job opportunity outreach.
